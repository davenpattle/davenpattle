    Welcome to NITK Racing!




    We are a team of undergraduate's from NITK Surathkal participating in the Formula Student challenge. This competition involves building a Formula race car for an avid auto-crosser.


    To our credit we have the lightest Indian FS car; the best Asian team at FS Hungary 2012; the fastest growing Indian team; and the only Indian car to be wind-tunnel tested!
