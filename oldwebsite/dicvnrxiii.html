The DICV NR-XIII

 

Efforts toward the NR-XIII, our car for the next season are in full swing. We concluded the design process in January 2013. Key additions this year include:

 

  1. 1.    Center feed intake system made of carbon-fibre
  2. 2.    Steering-mounted completely clutch-less electronic gear shifters for 75% faster gear changes
  3. 3.    Lower drive ratio for faster acceleration
  4. 4.    Pushrod suspension for improved packaging
  5. 5.    Hand actuated clutch for improved pedal assembly
  6. 6.    Use of rapid-prototyping process for manufacturing intake system components
  7. 7.    Targeted weight of 220 kg
  8. 8.    Multi-function displays on the steering wheel and dash-board.

 

For further updates on the manufacturing progress, please check the Updates and Gallery.
