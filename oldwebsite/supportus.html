SUPPORT US

Why?

We are undergraduates - all under the age of 22 - and have set out to build a high performance car, worthy of representing India globally. In the last year alone, over 60,000 man-hours were invested in the project.

Our achievements:

  1. 1.     Lightest Indian FS car in 2012, a feat achieved in just 2 years
  2. 2.     Top 10 spot in two events out of seven at Formula Student Hungary, 2012
  3. 3.     Best Asian team at Formula Student Hungary, 2012
  4. 4.     Research paper in SAE International journal of the work done on optimization of the cooling system
  5. 5.     Cabinet of trophies from performances at the Baja series

NITK Racing believes in developing a trend to shift outside the classroom learning paradigm. Supporting an international engineering project of this magnitude, helps facilitate industry-institute interaction, besides promoting innovation.
