TEAM NITK RACING

A Formula race car is a complex machine that requires multi-disciplinary specialization. Our team consists of students from over 8 engineering disciplines, who are actively involved in designing, building, testing, racing and marketing the car. To ease management and improve co-ordination, the team is organized based on the 5 principal sub-systems of the car.

We are mentored by 2 faculty members from the Department of Mechanical Engineering, NITK Surathkal.:

- Dr. Kumar G N, Assistant professor

- Mr. Suresh Kumar Y, Associate professor

The Engine Sub-system:

Engine sub-system handles the design, manufacture and testing of the engine, intake, exhaust, power transmission systems.

 

 

The Electronics Sub-system:

This sub-system handles all tasks associated with the electronics, wiring, battery, data acquisition and monitoring systems.

 

 

The Chassis Sub-system:

This sub-system work on designing, building and testing of the chassis, pedal assembly and the body shell.

 

 

The Suspension Sub-system:

This sub-system works on the designing, manufacturing and testing of the suspension, dampers, knuckles, steering mechanism and brake systems.

 

 

The Marketing Sub-system:

This sub-system works on fund raising, public relations, project management and brand building.
