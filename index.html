We are NITKRacing, a group of dedicated students from the National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal working towards the goal:

To participate in the Formula Student Series of competitions and achieve a top 10 spot and put our college on the world map and showcase Indian Engineering Excellence.
What do we do?
Create
We create a car from ground up, starting from nil to progressing towards an event ready car. Ranging from the design of chassis to the finishing touches on the car body.
Conduct
We conduct all the aspects that come with designing a formula racing car with vigour. Ranging from the technical aspect to marketing and media aspects.
Compete
We participate in FSAE events held all across the globe throughout the year to test the fruit of our efforts. We aim to put NITK on the global map.
